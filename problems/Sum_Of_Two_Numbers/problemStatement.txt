<p>Given two integers <i>A</i> and <i>B</i>, print the sum of <i>A</i> and <i>B</i>.</p>

<p><b>Input:</b><br/>
First line will contain an integer <i>T</i>, number of test cases.<br/>
Next <i>T</i> lines contains two integers <i>A</i> and <i>B</i>.<br/>
</p>
<p><b>Output:</b><br/>
For each test case, print the sum of the two integers <i>A</i> and <i>B</i>.<br/>
</p>
<p><b>Constraints:</b><br/>
1 <= <i>T</i> <= 100<br/>
1 <= <i>A</i> <= 10<br/>
1 <= <i>B</i> <= 10<br/>
</p>
